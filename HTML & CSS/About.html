About

Jamar Thrasher, CEO Kent Parker

Kent Parker is a social network for businesses seeking press coverage and media outlets offering press coverage. Kent Parker places an emphasis on online content on media outlets, as this content is easiest to be shared among large groups of people.

Kent Parker is a full media directory. Kent Parker's press release drafter helps you craft the perfect pitch. Kent Parker is a secure platform. Rank media outlets on various factors, such as willingness to publish your news.Use Kent Parker to scoop other media outlets, increase online visits, keep reporter information confidential, find story ideas, and provide feedback to newsmakers on their news nuggets based on various factors, such as newsworthiness.
